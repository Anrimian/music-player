**Cloud Synchronization**

The app offers optional cloud synchronization using third-party cloud storage services (Google Drive, Dropbox). When you enable cloud sync:

*   The app accesses your cloud storage account through official APIs (Google Drive API, Dropbox API) using OAuth 2.0 authorization.
*   The app reads and writes music files in your cloud storage folders. It also stores synchronization metadata in a `.smp-metadata` folder. It does not access files outside defined remote root folders.
*   Your music files and metadata are uploaded to and downloaded from your own cloud storage account. No data is sent to any server controlled by the developer.
*   OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any third party.
*   Synchronization action records (such as file additions, deletions, modifications, and moves) are stored locally on your device and in your cloud storage to enable proper sync conflict resolution. These records are retained for a limited period and are not accessible to the developer or any third party.

You can revoke cloud access at any time through the app settings or through your cloud provider's account permissions page.
